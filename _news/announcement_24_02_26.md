New preprint: [ECHOSAT: Estimating Canopy Height Over Space And Time](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.21421), the first global-scale height map that accurately quantifies tree growth and disturbances over time. We expect ECHOSAT to advance global efforts in carbon monitoring and disturbance assessment. Blogpost coming soon! In the meantime, check out the map on the [Google Earth Engine](https://ai4forest.projects.earthengine.app/view/echosat-temporal).
